Pasta

Description

This is how to make easy pasta. You pretty much just boil the pasta.

I have made lots of pastas in my lifetime. I am sure they are not as good as the ones in Italy. But it is pretty easy and quick to do.

Ingredients

  • Your Choice of Pasta Noodles
  • Butter
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put pasta into boiling water
  3. Boil until al dente
  4. Strain pasta and add butter
  5. Add pasta sauce
  6. Add salt and pepper
  7. Serve.
