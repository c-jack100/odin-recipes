Chili

Description

This is a test chili recipe I found online, but it looks pretty good. I may try it someday once I get done with all of this. Or I could stick to the one I have already done.

The one we made the other day was very good as well, so why change that one if it isnt broken? I can't remember exactly what it was though, just lots of onions.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Mixed Garlic
  • Bell Peppers
  • Spice Mixture
  • Tomatoes
  • Kidney Beans
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Make seasoning in bowl
  2. Brown beef with Garlic
  3. Add bell peppers and cook until soft
  4. Add rest of Ingredients
  5. Simmer
  6. Add salt and pepper
  7. Serve.
